inspiration came from the theme of being a sole survivor, as there is a lot more suspense with being on your own in many different ways. The main character (protagonist) is based to be on there own hopeless fighting for their lives.

vampire diaries is a good idea as it shows the main character (Elena) to be vulnerable as super natural life unfolds around her and she feels helpless as shes a mortal.

Sunderland from Silent Hill follows this theme too because he is also mortal fighting supernaturals.
